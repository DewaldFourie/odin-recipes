Chicken Breasts

Description

Crispy and juicy chicken breasts in the airfryer. Ready within only 12 minutes. For the healthy option this recipes is perfect.

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chicken Spice
  • 1 Lemon

Steps

  1. Rub the Chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Add Chicken Spice
  3. Airfry the Chicken Breasts at 180 degrees for 12 minutes
  4. Serve with lemon
